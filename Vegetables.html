Our Farms

Thambuththegama

This farm is part of Grocies' Sarubima program, which has transformed traditional farming with modern methods, increasing yields and reducing waste. The program has created 80 model farms, 35 of which are in Thambuththegama.

Thanamalwila

This farm is known for its community development initiatives, including a scholarship scheme for farmers’ children. The Grocies Sarubima Agriculture Modernisation project was launched here in 2008

Norochcholai

This farm is notable for its successful cultivation of various crops, including onions. It brings together Sinhalese-Tamil cultivators for a common purpose and is part of the 80 model farms created by the Sarubima program.

Kandepuhulpola

This is the location of the Grocies Vegetable Collecting Center, which plays a crucial role in the company’s supply chain. It ensures the regular incomes of farmers and contributes to the local economy.
